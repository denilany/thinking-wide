# Solution Overview – SkillBridge AI

SkillBridge AI is a simple web-based career guidance platform that helps users:
- Understand which careers fit their current skills
- Identify skill gaps for their desired roles
- Receive a personalized learning roadmap

## Key Features
1. Skill-to-Career Matching
Users input their current skills, education level, and interests.

2. AI Career Suggestions
The system suggests 3–5 suitable career paths with explanations.

3. Learning Roadmap
Each career includes a step-by-step skill roadmap with recommended learning resources.

4. Progress Tracking (Future)
Users can track completed skills and milestones.

## What Makes It Unique
- Focuses on clarity, not just job listings
- Beginner-friendly and accessible
- AI acts as a mentor, not just a search engine
